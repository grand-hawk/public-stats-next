# Modern

Added in the early days of MTC the Modern loadout covers today's main battlefield with main battle tanks, infantry fighting vehicles, and the rest of present-day kit. It lines up the conventional militaries of the [[/teams/hawk-republic Hawk Republic]] and [[/teams/eagle-federation Eagle Federation]] against paramilitary and insurgent outfits like the [[/teams/fish-state FISH State]]. The pace is fast.
