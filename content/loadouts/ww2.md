# WW2

WW2 is the second loadout in MTC. The tech of the era was more limited, so matches tend to run slower than in [[/loadouts/modern Modern]], but there's still a solid range of vehicle classes to suit different playstyles. The three teams here are [[/teams/hawk-republic Hawk]], [[/teams/eagle-federation Eagle]], and [[/teams/schwalbenheim-kingdom Schwalbenheim]].
