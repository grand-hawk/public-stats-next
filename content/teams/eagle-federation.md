# Eagle Federation

The **Eagle Federation** is one of the two original teams that have stood alongside the [[/teams/hawk-republic Hawk Republic]] since MTC 3. In the [[/loadouts/modern Modern]] loadout it represents NATO and the other U.S.-aligned nations. In [[/loadouts/ww2 World War 2]], it takes the role of the [Allied Powers](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Allies_of_World_War_II), mainly the United States, Great Britain, France, and their partners.
