# Felid Empire

The **Felid Empire** represents modern-day [Japan](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Japan) in the [[/loadouts/modern Modern]] loadout, with its vehicles playable in-game under the [[/teams/eagle-federation Eagle Federation]]. In [[/loadouts/ww2 World War 2]], the Felid Empire stands for the [Empire of Japan](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Empire_of_Japan) and the territories it conquered during the war.
