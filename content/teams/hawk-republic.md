# Hawk Republic

The **Hawk Republic** is the second of the two founding teams carried over from MTC 3. In the [[/loadouts/modern Modern]] loadout it represents real-world [Russia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Russia). In [[/loadouts/ww2 World War 2]], it takes the place of the [Soviet Union](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Soviet_Union).
