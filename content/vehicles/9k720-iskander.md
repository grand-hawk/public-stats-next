# 9K720 Iskander

## Description

The **9K720 Iskander** is Rocket Artillery on a wheeled transporter-erector-launcher modeled on Russia's short-range ballistic missile system that entered service in the mid-2000s as a more accurate, road-mobile successor to earlier Soviet tactical missiles; NATO refers to the missile family as **SS-26 Stone**. It is crewed by a driver, gunner, and commander, and the launcher weighs 42 tonnes. A **YaMZ-8424.10** diesel delivers 470 horsepower for 11.2 hp/t, allowing up to 80 km/h forward and 16 km/h in reverse with a nine-forward, two-reverse transmission.\
The turret mounts the SS-26 weapon system with two missile rounds and a six-second reload cycle, firing selectable HE-fragmentation warheads in low, medium, high, and direct modes. The gunner's sight spans 20x to 40x magnification and does not include a laser rangefinder, consistent with beyond-line-of-sight rocket employment.

This vehicle features an optional **THERMO-NUCLEAR 5KT WARHEAD** addon costing 50,000 points.
