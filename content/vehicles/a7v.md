# A7V

## Description

The **A7V** is a Heavy Tank patterned after Germany’s First World War **Sturmpanzerwagen A7V**, a boxy assault vehicle developed in 1917–1918 when the German Army rushed to field its own tracked breakthrough tank; only a handful saw combat before the Armistice, but the design became the enduring symbol of early German armour. In-game it is a tracked, non-amphibious vehicle weighing 30 tonnes and operated by a very large crew spanning driver, commander, main gunner, multiple bow gunners, loaders, and ammunition handlers. Twin **2x Daimler-Mercedes** diesel engines deliver 200 horsepower for a modest 6.7 hp/t, with a three-speed manual transmission offering 12 km/h forward, 6 km/h reverse, and neutral steering to help pivot the long hull.\
The primary armament is a **57 mm Maxim-Nordenfelt** cannon in a limited-traverse mount, directed through a **Telescope sight** with selectable 4× magnification and no rangefinder or fire-control computer. Six additional **7.72 Lewis 1916** machine guns sit in separate mounts; most of those positions use stabilized gunnery aids and simple optics, while the main gun relies on manual laying typical of the era.
