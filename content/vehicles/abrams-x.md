# Abrams X

## Description

The **Abrams X** is a Main Battle Tank and a technology demonstrator for the next generation of the **M1 Abrams** family, unveiled by General Dynamics Land Systems to showcase reduced crew count, hybrid power, and modular armament. The in-game version reflects a three-crew layout — driver, gunner, and commander — with no loader: the **120mm XM360** is fed by an autoloader. Weighing 57.7 tonnes on a tracked chassis, it is powered by an **XAP-1000** diesel producing 1,500 horsepower for a 26 hp/t power-to-weight ratio and a top speed of 72 km/h in both directions.\
The gunner has a stabilized main gun with laser rangefinder, fire control system with lead and tracking, and Gen 3 white thermals; the commander operates a stabilized **30mm M230LF** chain gun and a **Loitering Drone** launcher, both with independent laser and thermals. An active protection system with three launchers (three charges each) intercepts incoming projectiles between 70 and 1,000 m/s, and the vehicle is fitted with a laser warning system, missile approach warning system, and smoke grenades.
