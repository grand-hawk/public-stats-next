# Achzarit Mk.2 (RCWS-30)

## Description

The **Achzarit Mk.2 (RCWS-30)** is an infantry fighting vehicle, a heavy Israeli modification of Soviet-built T-54/T-55 tanks fitted with the Samson Remote Controlled Weapon Station (RCWS) (also known as Katlanit). It weighs 45.4 t with a crew of three and seven passengers, and is tracked. The **8V-92TA** diesel engine produces 720 hp for 15.9 hp/t and a top speed of 55 km/h forwards and 55 km/h in reverse, with neutral steering.
The turret is fully featured and includes lead, tracking, Gen 3 thermals, and a stabilizer. It utilises the **30mm Treemaster**, a **7.62 mm FN MAG 60-40** coaxial machine gun, and **Spike-LR2/MR** FNF ATGMs. The driver is equipped with 12 smoke grenades.
