# Ariete C1

## Description

The **Ariete C1** is a Main Battle Tank and the primary MBT of the Italian Army, developed by a consortium led by Iveco and Oto Melara and entering service in the late 1990s. It was designed to replace the Leopard 1 and M60 in Italian service with a balance of firepower, mobility, and protection suited to Italy's terrain; criticism of its armor led to the nickname reflected in its in-game description. The vehicle is crewed by four — driver, gunner, commander, and loader — on a tracked hull weighing 54 tonnes, powered by a **Fiat MTCA 12V** diesel producing 1,300 horsepower for a 24.1 hp/t ratio, with a top speed of 65 km/h forward and 46 km/h reverse and an automatic transmission with neutral steering.\
Main armament is the **120mm OTO Breda 120/44** smoothbore cannon, fed by the loader and supplemented by **7.62mm Beretta MG42/59** machine guns in coaxial, commander, and loader positions. The turret is stabilized and fitted with a laser warning system, smoke grenades, a laser rangefinder, Gen 2 green thermals, and a fire control system with lead computation at 10x magnification.
