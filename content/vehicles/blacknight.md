# Black Night

## Description

The **Black Night** is a Main Battle Tank and an advanced Challenger 2-derived variant fitted with the **Streetfighter II** remote weapon station and an active protection system. The name refers to the British Army's Challenger 2 upgrade program and night-fighting capability. The 65 t tracked vehicle is crewed by four — driver, gunner, commander, and loader — and reaches 56 km/h forward and 38 km/h reverse. The **Perkins CV12** diesel produces 1,314 hp for 20.2 hp/t.\
Main armament is the **120mm L30A1** with L27A1, L31A7, and L34A2, plus a coaxial **7.62 mm L94A1** and a loader **7.62mm L37A2**. The vehicle has an APS (2 launchers, 2 charges each), stabilizer, and smoke grenades. The gunner has a laser rangefinder, 3x–10x day and Gen 3 thermal sights with FCS and lead; the commander CITV has Gen 3 thermals and tracking. An engine smoke system is fitted.
