# BMD-4M

## Description

The **BMD-4M** is an Infantry Fighting Vehicle and a modernized **BMD-4** (BMD-3 derivative) for Russian airborne forces, with a **Bakhcha-U** turret mounting a 100 mm gun/launcher and 30 mm autocannon. The design keeps the BMD lineage’s air-droppable, amphibious layout while adding firepower comparable to the **BMP-3**. The 15 t tracked hull reaches 70 km/h on land and 4 km/h afloat, with a **UTD-29** diesel producing 500 hp (33.3 hp/t), active suspension, neutral steering, and a crew of three plus five passengers.\
Main armament is the **100mm 2A70M** (9M117M1 ATGM and HE) and **30mm 2A72** plus **7.62mm PKT**, with stabilizer, laser rangefinder, FCS with lead and tracking, Gen 2 thermals, and a commander CITV. Smoke grenades and an engine smoke system are fitted; optional add-ons include extra armour and airdrop kit.
