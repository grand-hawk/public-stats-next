# Abandoned IFV Hull

## Description

The **Abandoned IFV Hull** is an Armoured Personnel Carrier presented as a lightweight wheeled platform of uncertain origin, in-game represented as an abandoned IFV hull repurposed for transport. Weighing 10 t with seating for a driver and four passengers, it is powered by a **UTD-20** diesel producing 300 hp for a 30 hp/t power-to-weight ratio and a top speed of 65 km/h. The vehicle has no turret or integral armament in its basic configuration and relies on mobility and a low profile for survival. Locomotion is wheeled and non-amphibious.
