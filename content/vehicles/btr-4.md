# BTR-4

## Description

The **BTR-4** is an Armoured Personnel Carrier developed by Ukraine in the 2000s, featuring an 8×8 layout, a **30mm 2A72** cannon in a stabilized turret, and optional **Stugna-P** ATGM. It was designed for troop transport, fire support, and scouting and has been used in Ukrainian service. The 28 t wheeled vehicle carries a driver, gunner, commander, and eight passengers and is amphibious at 4 km/h, with a top speed of 110 km/h. The **3TD-4** diesel produces 600 hp for 21.4 hp/t and an automatic gearbox. The **30mm 2A72** and **7.62mm PKT** are in a stabilized turret with smoke grenades; the Stugna-P is a separate ATGM station. The gunner has a laser rangefinder, Gen 2 thermals, 4x–12x zoom, and FCS with lead.
