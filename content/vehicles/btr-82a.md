# BTR-82A

## Description

The **BTR-82A** is an Armoured Personnel Carrier and a modernized variant of the Soviet-era BTR-80, with a new **30mm 2A72** cannon in a stabilized turret and improved optics. It entered Russian service in the 2010s and is widely used by Russian and allied forces. The 15 t wheeled vehicle carries a driver, gunner, commander, and six passengers and is amphibious at 9 km/h, with a top road speed of 90 km/h. The **KamAZ 740.14-300** diesel produces 300 hp for 20 hp/t.\
The **30mm 2A72** and **7.62mm PKT** are in a stabilized turret with smoke grenades. The gunner has a laser rangefinder and FCS with 6x–19x zoom; the commander has a stabilized sight with 15x laser. Light armour and mobility are balanced for the APC role.
This vehicle features a single available upgrade: **Improved Ammunition**. Costing 500 points, this addon upgrades the stock 3URB8 APDS to 3URB11 APFSDS, increasing maximum penetration by 15mm (from 82mm to 97mm).
