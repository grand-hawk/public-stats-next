# Challenger 2 Medusa

## Description

The **Challenger 2 Medusa** is a Main Battle Tank variant of the British Challenger 2, adding laser warning, missile approach warning, and DIRCM for improved survivability against guided threats. The base Challenger 2 entered British service in 1998 and remains in use with upgraded optics and armour. The 64 t tracked vehicle is crewed by four — driver, gunner, commander, and loader — and reaches 56 km/h forward and 38 km/h reverse. The **Perkins CV12** diesel produces 1,314 hp for 20.5 hp/t.\
Main armament is the **120mm L30A1** rifled gun with L31A7, L26A1, and L34A2 ammunition, plus a coaxial **7.62 mm L94A1**. The gunner has a laser rangefinder, 3x–10x day sight, Gen 2 thermal sight, and 10x backup sight; LWS and MAWS are fitted. Stabilizer and smoke grenades are standard, along with an engine smoke system.
This vehicle features two addons: **Dorchester 2E** and **L27A1**. The Dorchester 2E Add-on costs 250 points and equips the Challenger 2 Medusa with additional ERA on the LFP and the hull sides; increasing overall protection for the hull. The L27A1 addon costs 500 points and replaces the stock L26A1 with the L27A1 APFSDS; this shell offers a 97mm increase in maximum penetration (from 467mm to 564mm), significantly boosting the overall lethality of the tank.
