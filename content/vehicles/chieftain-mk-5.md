# Chieftain Mk.5

## Description

The **Chieftain Mk.5** is a Main Battle Tank, the definitive production variant of the British Chieftain series that served from the 1960s into the 1990s, notable for its reclining driver position and **120mm L11A5** rifled gun. It weighs 55 tonnes with a crew of four — driver, gunner, commander, and loader — and is tracked. The **Leyland L60** multi-fuel engine delivers 760 hp for 13.8 hp/t and a top speed of 48 km/h, with neutral steering.\
The main gun is stabilized and supported by a **12.7 mm L21A1** ranging machine gun and coaxial **7.62mm L8A1**; the turret has smoke grenades and a fire control system with 8x main and 7x backup sights. The commander has a stabilized **7.62mm L37A1** and 1x–10x sight.
