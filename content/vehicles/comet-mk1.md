# Comet Mk.1

## Description

The **Comet Mk.1** is a Medium Tank, a British cruiser tank that entered service in late 1944 and combined the Cromwell’s mobility with a more powerful **77mm OQF Mk.II** gun derived from the 17-pounder. It weighs 33.5 tonnes with a crew of five — driver, gunner, bow gunner, commander, and loader — and is tracked. The **Rolls-Royce Meteor Mark III** produces 600 hp for 17.9 hp/t and a top speed of 52 km/h, with neutral steering and an engine smoke system.\
The main gun is complemented by coaxial and bow **7.92 mm BESA** machine guns; the gunner has no stabilizer or rangefinder. Lower profile and improved armament made it an effective late-war cruiser.
