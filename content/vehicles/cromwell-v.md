# Cromwell V

## Description

The **Cromwell V** is a Medium Tank, the main production variant of the British Cromwell cruiser tank that entered service in 1944 and served in Normandy and beyond. It is a tracked vehicle weighing 28 tonnes with a crew of five — driver, gunner, bow gunner, commander, and loader. The **Rolls-Royce Meteor Mark III** delivers 600 hp for 21.4 hp/t and a top speed of 52 km/h, with neutral steering and an engine smoke system.\
Main armament is the **75mm OQF Mk.V** with a coaxial **7.92 mm BESA**; the gunner has no stabilizer or rangefinder. An RP-3 rocket addon is available for indirect fire support.
This vehicle features a single addon: **RP-3 Rockets**. The RP-3 Rockets addon costs 500 points and equips the Cromwell V with four turret-mounted RP-3 rockets. Each rocket features 46 mm of maximum penetration and 5.44 kg of explosive mass, making them highly effective against light vehicles and infantry. However, because the launchers are fixed to the turret, the rockets cannot be aimed, which reduces their overall versatility in hilly or uneven terrain.
