# Cromwell V

## Description

The **Cromwell V** is a Medium Tank, the main production variant of the British Cromwell cruiser tank that entered service in 1944 and served in Normandy and beyond. It is a tracked vehicle weighing 28 tonnes with a crew of five — driver, gunner, bow gunner, commander, and loader. The **Rolls-Royce Meteor Mark III** delivers 600 hp for 21.4 hp/t and a top speed of 52 km/h, with neutral steering and an engine smoke system. Main armament is the **75mm OQF Mk.V** with a coaxial **7.92 mm BESA**; the gunner has no stabilizer or rangefinder. An RP-3 rocket addon is available for indirect fire support.
