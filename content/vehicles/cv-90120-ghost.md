# CV 90120-T Ghost

## Description

The **CV 90120-T Ghost** is a light tank armed with a 120 mm cannon. The CV 90 family entered service in the 1990s and is used by Sweden and several export customers; the 90120-T variant emphasises anti-tank capabilities and was a further development of the CV 90105 after the manufacturer saw its success. This specific variant is a technology demonstrator developed by BAE Systems with the **ADAPTIV** thermal camouflage system; this allows for the majority of the tank to be invisible to thermals. The 35 t vehicle carries a crew of four and reaches 70 km/h forward and 45 km/h reverse. The **Scania DSI14** diesel produces 550 hp for 23.4 hp/t and features an automatic gearbox.
The **120mm LLR L/47** is an autoloaded 120 mm cannon featuring a 4.3 s reload and is paired with a **12.7mm ksp 88**. The gunner has a stabiliser, laser rangefinder, Gen 3 thermals, 3x and 10.5x zoom, and FCS with lead and tracking. The CV 90120-T has 12 turret ammo and 33 hull ammo.
This vehicle features a single add-on: XH 45. The XH 45 costs 500 points and is an ammo add-on which replaces the stock DM53 APFSDS with XH 45; this new shell boasts higher penetration, mass, and velocity, with the max pen going from 658 mm for the DM53 to 732 mm for the XH 45.
