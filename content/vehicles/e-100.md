# E-100 (Henschelturm)

## Description

The **E-100** is a Super Heavy Tank and a variant fitted with a lighter turret to reduce total mass. The E-100 was a late-World War II German development by Adler as part of the Entwicklung series of standardized heavy designs; only one chassis was built and the project was abandoned before completion. In-game, the E-100 is a tracked vehicle weighing 100 tonnes, crewed by five — driver, gunner, bow gunner, commander, and loader. The **MB HL234** diesel engine produces 900 horsepower for a 9 hp/t power-to-weight ratio, with a top speed of 36 km/h forward and 10 km/h in reverse; neutral steering aids maneuvering.\
Primary armament is the **128 mm L/66** cannon (PzGr 43 and Sprgr. L/5), with a 19.3 s reload and 26 rounds stowed; the gunner has a 3x periscope and a 15x optical rangefinder sight. Secondary armament includes two **7.92 mm MG34** machine guns — one coaxial in the main turret and one in the commander’s cupola, the latter on a stabilized mount — plus a loader-operated **Grenade Mortar** with frag and smoke rounds for screening.
