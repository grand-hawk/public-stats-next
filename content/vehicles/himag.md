# HIMAG

## Description

The **HIMAG** (High Mobility Agility) is a Light Tank and American test bed from the 1970s–80s, built to explore advanced suspension and powerpack technology for future armored vehicles; it was never intended for combat and is extremely lightly armored. Weighing 40 tonnes with a crew of three — driver, gunner, and commander — it is tracked and fitted with active suspension (ride height, lateral roll, and pitch). The **AVCR-1360** diesel delivers 1,500 hp for 37.5 hp/t and a top speed of 96 km/h.\
The **75mm ADMAG** automatic cannon is stabilized and fed by an autoloader; the gunner has a laser rangefinder, fire control with lead, and Gen 2 white-hot thermals at 2x and 8x zoom. The commander has a stabilized CITV with laser and thermals (2x–8x).
