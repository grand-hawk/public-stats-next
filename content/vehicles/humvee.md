# Humvee

## Description

The **Humvee** (High Mobility Multipurpose Wheeled Vehicle) is a Transport Vehicle, the ubiquitous American 4×4 light truck that entered service in the 1980s and has served in conflicts worldwide. In-game it weighs 2.7 tonnes and carries five — driver, gunner, and three passengers — with a **YaMZ-5347-10** diesel producing 215 hp for an exceptional 79.6 hp/t and a top speed of 90 km/h.\
The gunner operates a stabilized **12.7mm M2** machine gun in an open ring mount with 1x sight. As a wheeled transport it prioritizes speed and payload over protection.
