# Humvee

## Description

The **Humvee** (High Mobility Multipurpose Wheeled Vehicle) is a Transport Vehicle, the ubiquitous American 4×4 light truck that entered service in the 1980s and has served in conflicts worldwide. In-game it weighs 2.7 tonnes and carries five — driver, gunner, and three passengers — with a **YaMZ-5347-10** diesel producing 215 hp for an exceptional 79.6 hp/t and a top speed of 90 km/h.\
The gunner operates a stabilized **12.7mm M2** machine gun in an open ring mount with 1x sight. As a wheeled transport it prioritizes speed and payload over protection.
This vehicle features three addons: **I-TOW**, **MK19** and **No Trunk**. The I-TOW addon costs 1,000 points and replaces the stock 12.7mm M2 machine gun with the BGM-71C I-TOW ATGM launcher. This enables the Humvee to engage enemy main battle tanks, as the BGM-71C features 800 mm of maximum penetration. The MK19 addon costs 500 points and replaces the 12.7mm M2 with a 40mm MK19 AGS; this system utilizes M383 HE shells, which are perfect for suppressing and countering enemy infantry. Finally, the "No Trunk" addon is free and removes the rear trunk cover.
