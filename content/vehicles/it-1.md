# IT-1

## Description

The **IT-1** is an Anti Tank Missile Carrier, a Soviet design that mounted the **3M7 Drakon** (Dragon) ATGM system in a fully enclosed turret on a **T-62** chassis. It entered limited service in the late 1960s as one of the first dedicated tank-based ATGM vehicles; the gun was removed in favour of a missile-only armament. In-game it is a tracked vehicle weighing 34.5 tonnes with a crew of three, powered by a **ChTZ V-55V** diesel producing 580 horsepower for 16.8 hp/t and 50 km/h forward.\
The main armament is the 3M7 Drakon with a 10-second reload and 12 rounds in the turret; a coaxial **7.62mm PKT** provides close defense. The gunner and commander turrets are stabilized, and the vehicle has an engine smoke system. The gunner sight has an 8x FCS with no rangefinder or thermals.
