# KF41 Lynx

## Description

The **KF41 Lynx** is an Infantry Fighting Vehicle developed by Rheinmetall as a heavier, more capable successor to the Puma family. The Lynx entered development in the 2010s and is offered in various configurations for export. In-game it is a tracked vehicle weighing 44 tonnes with a crew of three plus six passengers, powered by a **Liebherr D976** diesel producing 1,140 horsepower for 25.9 hp/t and 70 km/h in both directions.\
The gunner turret mounts a **30mm MK 30-2/ABM** cannon, a coaxial **7.62mm M240**, and **Spike-LR2MR** ATGMs; the commander has a **12.7mm M2**. The vehicle has an active protection system, laser and missile approach warning, stabilizer, smoke grenades, and a fire control system with lead and tracking plus third-generation thermals and laser rangefinder. The commander’s independent sight also has Gen 3 thermals and FCS.
