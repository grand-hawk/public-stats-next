# Kornet D BMD-4M

## Description

The **Kornet D BMD-4M** is an Anti Tank Missile Carrier based on the **BMD-4M** chassis, with the main turret replaced by a dedicated **9M133 Kornet** ATGM launcher. The “D” denotes a vehicle-mounted Kornet-D system for long-range anti-tank work. In-game it weighs 15 tonnes with a crew of three and is tracked and amphibious (4 km/h), with active suspension. The **UTD-29** diesel produces 500 hp for 33.3 hp/t and 70 km/h.\
The Kornet launcher has eight ready ATGMs and 16 reloads (9M133 and 9M133F-1) with 4 s reload and 7 s refill. The gunner has dual sights with laser rangefinder, FCS with lead and tracking, and Gen 2 white thermals at 6x–19x. The turret is stabilised and has smoke grenades; the vehicle has an engine smoke system.
