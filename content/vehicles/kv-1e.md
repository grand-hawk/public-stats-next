# KV-1E

## Description

The **KV-1E** is a Heavy Tank, an up-armoured **KV-1** variant; the “E” (Ekranami) suffix denotes appliqué armour plates added to the hull and turret. These vehicles were built in 1941 to improve protection against German anti-tank guns. In-game it is a tracked vehicle weighing 49 tonnes with a crew of five — driver, gunner, bow gunner, commander, and loader — and a **V-2K** diesel producing 600 horsepower for 12.2 hp/t and a top speed of 34 km/h. The main armament is the **76mm F-34** with OF-350M and BR-350B and a 7-second reload; **7.62mm DT-28** machine guns are mounted coaxially and in the loader and bow positions. The loader and bow gunner turrets are stabilized. Sights are 2.5x telescope and periscope with no rangefinder.
