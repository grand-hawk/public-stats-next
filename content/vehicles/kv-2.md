# KV-2

## Description

The **KV-2** is an Assault Gun based on the Soviet **KV-1** heavy tank chassis, fitted with a massive box-shaped turret mounting a **152mm M-10T** howitzer for destroying fortifications and strongpoints. It saw limited use in the Winter War and early Second World War; its tall silhouette and slow turret traverse made it vulnerable, but the 152 mm round was devastating when it hit. In-game it is a tracked vehicle weighing 52.4 tonnes with a crew of five and a **V-2K** diesel producing 600 horsepower for 11.5 hp/t and 34 km/h top speed.\
The main gun fires PB-35 and OF-530 with a 33-second reload; a coaxial **7.62mm DT-28** and DT-28s in the loader and bow positions provide close-in defense. The loader and bow gunner turrets are stabilized. Sights are 2.5x telescope and periscope with no rangefinder.
