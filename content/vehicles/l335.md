# L3/35

## Description

The **L3/35** is a Light Tank of Italian origin, officially the Carro Armato L3/35, used in large numbers during the 1930s and Second World War for reconnaissance and colonial duties. Its two-man crew and twin machine-gun or 20 mm armament made it obsolete against proper tanks. In-game it weighs 3.2 tonnes with a crew of two and is tracked. The **FIAT-SPA CV3** diesel produces 43 hp for 13.4 hp/t and 42 km/h.\
Armament is a **20mm Solothurn S-18/1000** (Paulina AP-T) and an **8mm Breda M37** machine gun; the gunner has 2x and 2.75x sights and a stabilised turret with ±15° elevation. There are no thermals, laser rangefinder, or smoke systems.
