# LAV-25

## Description

The **LAV-25** is an Armoured Personnel Carrier and the primary infantry variant of the US LAV (Light Armoured Vehicle) family, armed with a **25mm M242 Bushmaster** and used for reconnaissance and mechanised infantry. The LAV-25 entered USMC service in the 1980s and has seen wide export. In-game it weighs 12.9 tonnes with seating for nine (driver, gunner, commander, six passengers) and is wheeled and amphibious (4 km/h). The **Detroit 6V53T** diesel produces 300 hp for 23.3 hp/t and 100 km/h.\
The Bushmaster has 210-round magazines (M792 and M791); a coaxial and commander’s **7.62mm M240** provide backup. The gunner has an 8x sight only; the commander’s turret is stabilised with 1x. Smoke grenades are fitted.
