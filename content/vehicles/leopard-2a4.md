# Leopard 2A4

## Description

The **Leopard 2A4** is a Main Battle Tank and the most numerous production variant of the German Leopard 2, in service since the mid-1980s with the Bundeswehr and many NATO and allied nations. It introduced a welded turret with improved armour and the **120mm Rh120 L/44** as standard. In-game it is a 55.4 t tracked vehicle crewed by four. The **MTU MB 873 Ka-501** diesel produces 1,500 hp for 27.1 hp/t and 72 km/h forward (31 km/h reverse), with neutral steering and an automatic transmission. Main armament is the **120mm Rh120 L/44** with DM12 and DM33, plus a coaxial and loader’s **7.62mm MG3**. The gunner has a laser rangefinder, FCS with lead, and Gen 1 green thermals at 12x; the commander has a laser CITV at 2x and 8x. The turret is stabilised and carries smoke grenades.
