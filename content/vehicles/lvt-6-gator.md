# LVT-6 Gator

## Description

The **LVT-6 Gator** is an Amphibious Vehicle and a post–Second World War development of the Landing Vehicle Tracked family, used for riverine and coastal operations and infantry support. The LVT lineage saw extensive use in the Pacific. In-game it weighs 14.9 tonnes and is tracked and amphibious (4 km/h in water), with a crew of seven — driver, gunner, commander, loader, and three MG gunners. The **Continental W-670-9A** diesel produces 250 hp for 16.8 hp/t and 40 km/h on land.\
Primary armament is an **M2 Howitzer** (75 mm pack howitzer) with M48 and M66 ammunition; the commander and three MG gunners each have a **12.7mm M2** machine gun. The main turret has a 3x telescope only; the commander and MG turrets are stabilised. There is no laser rangefinder or modern FCS.
