# M1126

## Description

The **M1126** is an Armoured Personnel Carrier and the baseline infantry carrier of the **Stryker** family of 8×8 wheeled combat vehicles, fielded by the United States from the 2000s for rapid deployment and urban operations. It carries a full squad plus crew while staying within C-130 transport limits. Crewed by a driver, gunner, and commander with space for eight passengers, it weighs 16.5 tonnes and is powered by a **Caterpillar 3126** diesel producing 350 horsepower for 21.2 hp/t and 96 km/h.\
The gunner operates a stabilized **12.7mm M2** in a remote weapon station (CROWS) with a fire control system, lead and tracking, and Gen 2 thermals. Smoke grenades and optional slat armor and Javelin mounts are available; the vehicle relies on speed and situational awareness rather than heavy armor.
