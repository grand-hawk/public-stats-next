# M17 Whizbang

## Description

The **M17 Whizbang** is a Medium Tank based on the **M4 Sherman** chassis, armed with the **7.2 in T37** rocket launcher in addition to the standard **75 mm M3** gun, providing both direct-fire and area saturation capability in the style of the better-known Calliope. The nickname "Whizbang" came from the sound of the rockets. The 30.6 t vehicle is crewed by five (driver, gunner, bow gunner, commander, loader) and is powered by a **Continental R975-C4** radial engine producing 460 hp for 15 hp/t and 39 km/h. The **75 mm M3** fires M61 and M48; the **7.2 in T37** carries 20 rockets. A coaxial and bow **.30 cal M1919** complete the armament. The gunner has a stabilizer and 3x telescope plus 1x/6x periscope with no rangefinder or FCS; commander and bow positions are stabilized.
