# M1IP

## Description

The **M1IP** (Improved Performance) is a Main Battle Tank representing an upgraded **M1 Abrams** with improved turret armour and other modifications introduced in the mid-1980s before the 120 mm M1A1. The baseline M1 entered U.S. service in 1980 with a 105 mm gun and a gas turbine engine. Weighing 55.7 tonnes, it is crewed by four — driver, gunner, commander, and loader — and is tracked. The **Honeywell AGT1500** turbine produces 1,519 horsepower for 27.3 hp/t, 67 km/h forward and 38 km/h reverse, with neutral steering and an automatic transmission.\
Main armament is the **105 mm M68A1** rifled gun with APFSDS, HEAT, and HEP; a coaxial **7.62 mm M240** and a cupola **12.7 mm M2** provide secondary fire, and the loader has a **7.62 mm M240**. The turret has a stabilizer, a fire control system, a laser rangefinder, and Gen 1 green thermals (3x/9.8x and 8.6°/2.58° thermal fields of view). Smoke grenades and an engine smoke system are fitted.
