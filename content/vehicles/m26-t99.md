# M26 T99

## Description

The **M26 T99** is a Heavy Tank based on the **M26 Pershing**, fitted with the T99 rocket launcher — a calliope-style rack of **M8** rockets for area saturation, similar in concept to the Sherman Calliope. It combined the Pershing's **90 mm M3** gun and armour with a 44-rocket salvo capability for use against infantry and soft targets. The 41.6 t vehicle is crewed by five (driver, gunner, bow gunner, commander, loader) and is powered by a **Ford GAF** engine producing 500 hp for 12 hp/t and 48 km/h.\
The **90 mm M3** is the primary armament with M82, M304, and M71 ammunition; the **M8 Rocket** launcher carries 44 HE rockets. A coaxial **.30 cal M1919**, commander's **12.7 mm M2**, and bow **.30 cal M1919** complete the armament. The gunner has 4x/8x and 1x/6x sights with no rangefinder or FCS; the commander and bow positions have stabilizers.
