# M2A4E1

## Description

The **M2A4E1** is an Infantry Fighting Vehicle representing an upgraded **M2 Bradley** with improved powerpack, optics, and active protection. The M2 Bradley entered U.S. service in the 1980s to carry infantry and support them with a 25 mm cannon and TOW missiles; the A4 and E1 variants add reliability and survivability improvements. Weighing 35.9 tonnes, it carries a driver, gunner, commander, and six passengers and is tracked. The **VTA903E-T675** diesel produces 675 horsepower for 18.8 hp/t, 66 km/h top speed, neutral steering, and an automatic transmission.\
Armament includes the **25 mm M242 Bushmaster** autocannon, a **7.62 mm M240** machine gun, and **BGM-71F TOW 2A/B** ATGMs. The turret has a stabilizer, fire control with lead and tracking, laser rangefinder, and Gen 3 white thermals; the commander has a separate thermal CITV. An APS with two launchers (two charges each) can intercept threats between 70 and 2,000 m/s, with laser and missile approach warning. An engine smoke system is fitted; no IED or drone jammer is listed.
