# M2A4E1

## Description

The **M2A4E1** is an Infantry Fighting Vehicle representing an upgraded [[/vehicles/m2a1-bradley M2 Bradley]] with improved powerpack, optics, and active protection. The [[/vehicles/m2a1-bradley M2 Bradley]] entered U.S. service in the 1980s to carry infantry and support them with a 25 mm cannon and TOW missiles; the A4 and E1 variants add reliability and survivability improvements. Weighing 35.9 tonnes, it carries a driver, gunner, commander, and six passengers and is tracked. The **VTA903E-T675** diesel produces 675 horsepower for 18.8 hp/t, 66 km/h top speed, neutral steering, and an automatic transmission.\
Armament includes the **25 mm M242 Bushmaster** autocannon, a **7.62 mm M240** machine gun, and **BGM-71F TOW 2A/B** ATGMs. The turret has a stabilizer, fire control with lead and tracking, laser rangefinder, and Gen 3 white thermals; the commander has a separate thermal CITV. An APS with two launchers (two charges each) can intercept threats between 70 and 2,000 m/s, with laser and missile approach warning. An engine smoke system is fitted; no IED or drone jammer is listed.
This vehicle features two addons: **M3 Conversion** and **BRAT**. The M3 Conversion costs 250 points and trades two infantry seats for five additional TOW missiles, doubling the vehicle's internal capacity. The BRAT addon costs 500 points and reinforces the hull sides and Upper Frontal Plate (UFP) with ERA bricks to defend against chemical energy rounds and ATGMs.
