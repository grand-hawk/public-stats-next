# M3 GMC

## Description

The **M3 GMC** (Gun Motor Carriage) is a Tank Destroyer consisting of the **75 mm M1897A4** (a modernized French 75) mounted on the chassis of the **M3 half-track**. It was fielded in 1942 as an early U.S. tank destroyer before the M10 and M18 entered service. Weighing 9.1 tonnes, it is crewed by four — driver, gunner, commander, and loader — and is tracked. The **White 160AX** engine produces 148 horsepower for 16.3 hp/t and a top speed of 76 km/h. The only primary weapon is the **75 mm M1897A4** with HE and AP; there is no coaxial or anti-aircraft machine gun. The gun has limited traverse (6 deg/s horizontal) and a 1x telescope with no rangefinder. Light armor and an open fighting compartment keep weight down but expose the crew.
