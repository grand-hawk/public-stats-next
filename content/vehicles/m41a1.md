# M41A1

## Description

The **M41A1** is a Light Tank, the U.S. **M41 Walker Bulldog** family in its refined A1 configuration—developed in the early 1950s as a faster, better-armed successor to the M24 Chaffee and produced in large numbers for American and allied armies during the Cold War. In-game it is a tracked vehicle weighing 23.1 tonnes, crewed by four with separate driver, gunner, commander, and loader positions. An **AOS-895-3** diesel delivers 500 horsepower for a 21.6 hp/t power-to-weight ratio, with neutral steering and an automatic gearbox (four forward, one reverse gear) enabling up to 72 km/h forward and 19 km/h backward.\
The primary armament is a **76mm M32** cannon with ammunition stowed in ready rack, hull, and turret bins, supported by a coaxial **7.62mm M1919A4E1** machine gun. The gunner’s main sight offers 6× magnification with a fire control system but no rangefinder, backed by an 8× backup optic. The commander’s cupola mounts a stabilized **12.7mm M2** heavy machine gun on a quick-traversing mount, aimed through a simple 1× MG sight for close-in targets.
