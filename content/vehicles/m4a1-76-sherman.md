# M4A1 (76) Sherman

## Description

The **M4A1 (76) Sherman** is a Medium Tank combining the cast-hull M4A1 with the 76 mm M1 gun for improved anti-tank capability. Late-production M4A1s were fitted with the 76 mm in 1944–1945 to match heavier German armor in Europe. The vehicle weighs 32 tonnes and is crewed by five: driver, gunner, bow gunner, commander, and loader. The **Continental R975-C4** radial produces 400 horsepower for 12.5 hp/t and a top speed of 39 km/h. Main armament is the **76 mm M1** cannon with HE, AP, APCR, and HVAP ammunition; secondaries are a coaxial **.30 cal M1919**, cupola **12.7 mm M2**, and bow **.30 cal M1919**. The gunner, commander, and bow positions have stabilizers; the gunner uses a 4x/8x telescope and a 1x/6x periscope without a rangefinder.
