# M4A3E2 (75) Jumbo

## Description

The **M4A3E2 (75) Jumbo** is a Medium Tank and the original **M4A3E2 Jumbo**, an up-armoured assault variant of the **M4 Sherman** produced in 1944 for breaking through fortified positions. It saw notable use in the Battle of the Bulge and the push into Germany. The 37.8-tonne tracked vehicle is crewed by five — driver, gunner, bow gunner, commander, and loader — and is powered by a **Ford GAA V8** producing 500 hp for 13.2 hp/t and 35 km/h.\
The stabilized **75mm M3** is the main armament with coaxial **.30 cal M1919**; the commander has a **12.7mm M2** and the bow gunner a **.30 cal M1919**. The gunner has a 3x telescope and 1x/6x periscope with no rangefinder. The Jumbo's added armour made it significantly more resistant to anti-tank fire than the standard Sherman.
