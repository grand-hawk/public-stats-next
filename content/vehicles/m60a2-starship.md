# M60A2 Starship

## Description

The **M60A2 Starship** is a Main Battle Tank variant of the **M60** series, fielded by the United States in the 1970s with a low-profile turret and the **152 mm M162** gun-launcher capable of firing conventional rounds and the **MGM-51B Shillelagh** wire-guided missile. The combination was intended to engage armor at long range, but the system proved complex and the variant was phased out in favor of the 105 mm M60A3. The 51.9 t vehicle is crewed by four (driver, gunner, commander, loader) and is powered by an **AVDS-1790-2** diesel producing 750 hp for 14.5 hp/t and 48 km/h. The M162 can fire HEAT (M409A1), HE (M657A2, M625A1), and Shillelagh; a **7.62 mm M240** is coaxial and a **12.7 mm M85** is commander-mounted. The gunner has a laser rangefinder and FCS with 1x/8x magnification but no thermals; stabilizer and smoke grenades are fitted, and an engine smoke system is available.
