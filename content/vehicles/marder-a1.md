# Marder A1

## Description

The **Marder A1** is an Infantry Fighting Vehicle and the first major upgrade of the West German **Marder**, which entered service in 1971 and became a cornerstone of Bundeswehr mechanized infantry; the A1 modernized the fire control and powerpack while retaining the original turret and armament. The vehicle is tracked, weighs 29.1 tonnes, and carries a crew of three — driver, gunner, and commander — plus five passengers for a total of eight, and is powered by an **MTU MB 833 Ea-500** diesel engine producing 600 horsepower for a 20.6 hp/t power-to-weight ratio and a symmetric top speed of 75 km/h, with neutral steering and an automatic gearbox. The gunner operates a **20mm Rh202** cannon and a coaxial **7.62mm MG3**; the main gun has no stabilizer (as noted in-game) and the gunner uses 2× and 6× magnification without a laser rangefinder. A passenger can operate a second MG3 from a roof mount. Optional addons include **MILAN** ATGMs and a **Wärmeortungsempfänger** thermal imager. Smoke grenades and a 75 km/h reverse speed aid in disengagement.
