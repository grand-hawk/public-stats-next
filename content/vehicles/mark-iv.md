# Mark IV

## Description

The **Mark IV** is a Heavy Tank modeled on the British rhomboid tank family that entered service in 1917, when the Royal Tank Corps used “male” gun-armed and “female” machine-gun variants to cross trenches and suppress strongpoints on the Western Front. In-game, it is a tracked, non-amphibious hull weighing 28 tonnes and crewed by seven — two sponson gunners, three bow machine gunners, plus driver and commander. A **Ricardo** diesel is rated at 1500 horsepower, yielding an exceptional power-to-weight ratio on paper and a top speed of 6 km/h forward and 1 km/h in reverse across a three-speed manual box without neutral steering.\
Firepower is split between two **57 mm 6pdr OQF** guns in opposing sponsons, each paired with a **Telescope sight** offering stepped 2× magnification and no rangefinder or FCS. Three **7.72 Lewis 1916** machine guns cover the hull arcs; their mounts use in-game stabilizers and basic gunner optics distinct from the sponson telescopes.
