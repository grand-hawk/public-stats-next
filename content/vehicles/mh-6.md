# MH-6

## Description

The **MH-6** is a Utility Helicopter, the military designation for the **MD Helicopters MH-6 Little Bird**, a light single-engine scout and transport used extensively by the US Army's 160th Special Operations Aviation Regiment. The compact airframe allows operations from confined areas and fast insertion of small teams. In-game it is crewed by a pilot and co-pilot with seating for six passengers and has no base armament.\
An addon equips it with an **M134** minigun and **Hydra-70** rockets for armed escort or light attack. The pilot turret has a simple 0.7x sight with no rangefinder or fire control. The MH-6 trades payload and protection for agility and low signature, typical of special-operations light helicopters.
