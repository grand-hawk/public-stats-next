# Mi-24VM

## Description

The **Mi-24VM** is a Helicopter Gunship and a modernized export and Russian upgrade of the **Mi-24** "Hind," which has been one of the world’s most widely used attack helicopters since the 1970s; the VM variant brings improved avionics, a new gun mount, and optional guided weapons. The helicopter has a crew of two — pilot and co-pilot — and can carry up to 8 passengers for a total seating capacity of 10. The co-pilot operates a stabilized **23mm GSh-23L** cannon with a 450-round magazine (API-T and HEF-I), with a fire control system offering lead and tracking, laser rangefinder, and second-generation white-hot thermals at 3.7×–73.7× zoom. The pilot has a flare dispenser for countermeasures. Optional addons include the **9K127 Vikhr** or **9M120 Ataka** ATGMs, **9M39 Igla** air-to-air missiles, **S-8KOM** rockets, **B-13L** rocket pods, **UPK-23** gunpods, an exhaust cooler, and an IR jammer.
