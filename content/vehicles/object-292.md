# Object 292

## Description

The **Object 292** is a Main Battle Tank and a Russian experimental testbed based on the T-80 chassis, built to trial the **152mm LP-83** smoothbore gun; in reality the autoloader for this caliber proved problematic and the vehicle remained a technology demonstrator. The vehicle is tracked, weighs 46 tonnes, and is crewed by three — driver, gunner, and commander — and is powered by a **GTD-1000TF** gas turbine producing 1,100 horsepower for a 23.9 hp/t power-to-weight ratio and a top speed of 70 km/h.\
The main armament is the **152mm LP-83** with an autoloader (10-second cycle, 6-second refill) and HE and APFSDS ammunition; the turret is fully stabilized and served by a fire control system with lead computation and a laser rangefinder with 3.9×–9× zoom. The commander has a cupola with a 5× sight. An engine smoke system is fitted for defensive screening.
