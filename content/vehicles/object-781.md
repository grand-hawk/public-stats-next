# Object 781

## Description

The **Object 781** is a Fire Support Vehicle — a Soviet experimental tracked platform with multiple weapon stations, resembling a heavy IFV or fire-support APC. The design emphasizes firepower and crew capacity over armour. Weighing 46.4 tonnes, it carries a driver, commander, two gunners, three passengers, and multiple turrets. The **ChTZ V-84-1** diesel produces 840 hp for 18.1 hp/t and 65 km/h. Armament includes two **30mm 2A72** autocannon turrets with **7.62mm PKT** and **9M113 Konkurs** ATGMs on one turret; passenger positions have **7.62mm PKT** and a **30mm AGS-17** grenade launcher. All main stations have stabilizers, smoke grenades, and FCS; the commander has a cupola sight. An engine smoke system is fitted. The optional K1 Package adds further capability. The Object 781 functions as a multi-role fire support and infantry carrier with exceptional weapon density.
This vehicle features a single addon: **K1 Package**. The K1 Package addon costs 500 points and equips the hull sides with K1 ERA blocks; this significantly increases side protection against chemical energy (CE) rounds and ATGMs, making the vehicle much more resilient during urban combat or flanking maneuvers.
