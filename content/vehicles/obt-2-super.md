# OBT-2 Super

## Description

The **OBT-2 Super** is a Main Battle Tank and an upgraded **OBT-2** with enhanced cage or appliqué armour, reflected in the in-game "Super cage armour OBT-2" description. The vehicle is tracked, weighs 40 tonnes, and is crewed by four — driver, gunner, commander, and loader — and is powered by a **ChTZ V-55V** diesel engine producing 580 horsepower for a 14.5 hp/t power-to-weight ratio and a top speed of 50 km/h forward and 7 km/h in reverse. The main armament is the **100mm SB-10S** cannon with a 6-second reload and 9M117 ATGM, OF-412, and BR-412D ammunition, plus a **12.7mm DSHK** on the gunner’s turret and a second DSHK on the commander’s turret. The main gun has a two-plane stabilizer; the gunner uses 3.5×–7× magnification without a laser rangefinder, and the commander has a stabilizer and a 1× sight for the roof machine gun.
