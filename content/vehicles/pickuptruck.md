# MiKi Truck Company Model 1

## Description

The **MiKi Truck Company Model 1** is a Pickup Truck and a minimalist two-seat wheeled transport used to move personnel and light loads quickly between positions. Presented in-game as a compact civilian-style utility vehicle rather than an armoured fighting vehicle, it fits ad hoc motor pools and faction logistics where heavier trucks are unavailable or undesirable. It weighs 1.3 tonnes, seats a driver and one passenger, and is propelled by a **3VZ-E V6** diesel rated at 150 horsepower at up to 4,000 RPM, coupled to a five-forward, one-reverse transmission that yields a striking 118.1 hp/t power-to-weight ratio and a top speed of 110 km/h forward and 15 km/h in reverse.\
It mounts no stabilised weapons, smokescreens, or dedicated gunnery optics, so crews must depend on the chassis’s agility and low silhouette for survival on reconnaissance, engineer support runs, and battlefield taxi duties.
