# PlastunSN

## Description

The **PlastunSN** is a tracked Transport Vehicle — a light armoured personnel carrier capable of carrying six passengers plus driver and gunner, with optional armament. The design emphasizes low weight and high mobility for air-mobile or reconnaissance use. Weighing just 3.8 tonnes, it is crewed by a driver and gunner with seating for six passengers; an optional **12.7mm DSHK** mount can be fitted. The **VAZ-3416** diesel produces 80 hp for 21.1 hp/t and a top speed of 50 km/h. With minimal armour and no standard main armament, the PlastunSN relies on speed and small size to deliver or extract infantry; the airdrop addon allows deployment from aircraft for rapid insertion.
