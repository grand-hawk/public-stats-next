# Plastun SN

## Description

The **Plastun SN** is a tracked Transport Vehicle — a light armoured personnel carrier capable of carrying six passengers plus driver and gunner, with optional armament. The design emphasizes low weight and high mobility for air-mobile or reconnaissance use. Weighing just 3.8 tonnes, it is crewed by a driver and gunner with seating for six passengers. The **VAZ-3416** diesel produces 80 hp for 21.1 hp/t and a top speed of 50 km/h. With minimal armour and no standard main armament, the PlastunSN relies on speed and small size to deliver or extract infantry.
This vehicle features two add-ons: **12.7mm DSHK???** and **Airdrop**. The 12.7mm DSHK??? add-on costs 50 points and equips the Plastun SN with a modified 12.7mm DShK heavy machine gun, granting the gunner the necessary firepower to engage infantry and destroy obstructing foliage. The Airdrop add-on costs 1,000 points and allows the vehicle to deploy at almost any location on the map, enabling flanking maneuvers or the capture of distant enemy objectives.
