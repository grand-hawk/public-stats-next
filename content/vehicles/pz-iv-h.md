# Pz IV H

## Description

The **Pz IV H** is a Medium Tank and the main production variant of the Panzer IV, Germany’s most numerous tank of the Second World War; the Ausf. H entered service in 1943 with reinforced armour and the potent 75 mm L/48 gun. Weighing 26 tonnes, it is crewed by five: driver, gunner, bow gunner, commander, and loader. The **MB HL120 TRM** diesel produces 296 hp for 11.4 hp/t and a top speed of 39 km/h.\
Primary armament is the **75 mm KwK40 L48** with HE, PzGr 40, and PzGr 39, plus a coaxial **7.92mm MG34**; commander and bow gunners have stabilized **7.92mm MG34** mounts. The gunner’s telescope is 2.5x with no rangefinder or stabilizer; the vehicle relies on hull-down tactics and supporting infantry to offset its modest mobility and armour.
