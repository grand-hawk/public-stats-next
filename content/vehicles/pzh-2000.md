# PzH-2000

## Description

The **PzH-2000** is a Self-Propelled Howitzer, the German Panzerhaubitze 2000 that entered service in the late 1990s and is one of the most capable NATO 155 mm systems, with a high rate of fire and long range. In-game it is a tracked vehicle weighing 57 tonnes with a crew of five — driver, gunner, commander, loader, and one passenger. The **MTU MT881 Ka-500** diesel produces 1,000 horsepower (17.5 hp/t) for 62 km/h in both directions with neutral steering and an automatic gearbox.\
The **155mm L62** howitzer has a 3.14 s reload and a wide selection of charges and projectile types (including DM121 and DM702A1). The gunner has smoke grenades and an FCS with laser rangefinder and 8x zoom; the commander’s sight offers 2x–8x with laser.
This vehicle features two addons: **SMArt 155** and **Hedgehog Protection**. The SMArt 155 addon costs 1,000 points and introduces the SMArt 155 Smart Top-Attack Munition. This sophisticated system functions by launching two projectiles into a high-altitude trajectory over the target area; the munitions then deploy parachutes to glide downward, detonating upon detecting a vehicle within their lethal radius to strike the vulnerable roof armor—a mechanism functioning similarly to the TOW-2B. The Hedgehog Protection addon costs 250 points and equips the PzH-2000 with ERA tiles on the roof, providing critical defense against counter-battery fire and top-attack threats.
