# RBT-5

## Description

The **RBT-5** is a Light Tank, a Soviet interwar experiment that fitted the BT-5 with two **TT-250** “tank torpedoes” — large demolition rockets for breaching fortifications — in addition to its 45 mm gun. Only a handful were built. In-game it is a tracked vehicle weighing 11.5 tonnes with a crew of three (driver, gunner, loader). The **M-5-400** diesel produces 400 horsepower (34.8 hp/t) for 51 km/h.\
The gunner has the **45mm 20-K** cannon, a **7.62mm DT-28** machine gun, and two TT-250 rockets; the 2.5x telescope and periscope offer no rangefinder or stabilizer. The TT-250s provide a single heavy punch before the vehicle relies on the 45 mm and MG.
