# RBU-6000 MT-LB

## Description

The **RBU-6000 MT-LB** is a Rocket Artillery vehicle that mounts the Soviet **212mm RBU-6000** anti-submarine rocket system on an MT-LB chassis — a naval weapon repurposed for land-based area fire. In-game it is a tracked platform weighing 11.5 tonnes with a crew of three (driver, gunner, commander). The **YaMZ 238** diesel produces 240 horsepower (20.9 hp/t) for 62 km/h.\
The gunner fires RGB-60 rockets from a 12-round ready load with 20 in reserve; the commander has a **7.62mm PKT** for self-defence. Sights are basic (1x for the launcher, 2.6x for the commander); the vehicle is best used for indirect saturation rather than precision engagement.
