# Shturm-S

## Description

The **Shturm-S** is an Anti-Tank Missile Carrier, the Soviet 9P149 mounting the **9M114 Shturm** (AT-6 Spiral) radio-command ATGM in a twin launcher on an MT-LB-derived chassis. It entered service in the 1970s and remains in use. In-game it is a tracked, amphibious vehicle weighing 11.8 tonnes with a crew of two (driver and commander), capable of 6 km/h in water. The **YaMZ 238** diesel produces 240 horsepower (20.3 hp/t) for 62 km/h.\
The commander operates a stabilised launcher with 12 **9M114** missiles, with an 8x optical sight and a Gen 2 white-hot thermal sight, both linked to an FCS. An engine smoke system is fitted for defensive screening.
This vehicle features a single addon: **9M120 Ataka**. The 9M120 Ataka addon costs 1,000 points and replaces the stock 9M114 Shturm with the 9M120-1 and 9M120-1F Ataka ATGMs. The 9M120-1 Ataka boasts a massive 240 mm increase in maximum penetration over the stock missiles (jumping from 560 mm to 800 mm), allowing it to punch through the frontal armor of most contemporary main battle tanks. The 9M120-1F is the high-explosive (HE) variant, packing a substantial 6.9 kg of explosive mass for devastating anti-infantry and light-vehicle performance.
