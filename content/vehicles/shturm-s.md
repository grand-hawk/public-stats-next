# Shturm-S

## Description

The **Shturm-S** is an Anti-Tank Missile Carrier, the Soviet 9P149 mounting the **9M114 Shturm** (AT-6 Spiral) radio-command ATGM in a twin launcher on an MT-LB-derived chassis. It entered service in the 1970s and remains in use. In-game it is a tracked, amphibious vehicle weighing 11.8 tonnes with a crew of two (driver and commander), capable of 6 km/h in water. The **YaMZ 238** diesel produces 240 horsepower (20.3 hp/t) for 62 km/h. The commander operates a stabilised launcher with 12 **9M114** missiles, with an 8x optical sight and a Gen 2 white-hot thermal sight, both linked to an FCS. An engine smoke system is fitted for defensive screening.
