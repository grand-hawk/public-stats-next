# Sprut-SDM1

## Description

The **Sprut-SDM1** is a Light Tank — the Russian 2S25M Sprut-SDM1, an airborne assault gun mounting a 125 mm tank gun on a lightweight, amphibious chassis derived from the BMD family. It was developed to give airborne units anti-tank capability comparable to main battle tanks. In-game it weighs 18 tonnes, is crewed by three, and is amphibious at 10.6 km/h, with active suspension. The **UTD-29** diesel produces 500 hp for 27.8 hp/t and 70 km/h.\
The **125mm 2A75** is fed by an autoloader (APFSDS, **9M119M1** ATGM, HEAT, HE) with a coaxial **7.62mm PKT**; commander has a second PKT. Both gunner and commander have laser rangefinder, FCS with lead and tracking, and Gen 3 white thermals. Smoke grenades and engine smoke are fitted.
