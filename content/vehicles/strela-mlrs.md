# Strela-MLRS

## Description

The **Strela-MLRS** is a Rocket Artillery vehicle, a multiple rocket launcher on a compact tracked chassis; the name suggests a system related to Soviet-era Strela designations. In-game it is tracked, amphibious at 6 km/h, weighs 11.5 tonnes, and is crewed by three — driver, gunner, and commander. The **YaMZ 238** diesel produces 240 hp for 20.9 hp/t and 62 km/h.\
The main armament is the **UB-32** pod firing **S-5K1** rockets (68 rounds), with an FCS for the gunner; the commander has a **12.7mm NSVT**. Engine smoke is available. With light armour and exposed crew, it is intended for stand-off bombardment rather than direct combat.
