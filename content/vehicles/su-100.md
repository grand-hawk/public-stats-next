# SU-100

## Description

The **SU-100** is a Tank Destroyer that entered Soviet service in 1944, armed with the **100mm D-10S** gun on a lengthened T-34 hull to engage heavy German armour. It saw extensive use until the end of the war and in later conflicts. In-game it is a tracked vehicle weighing 31.6 tonnes with a crew of four — driver, gunner, commander, and loader. The **Model V-2-34** diesel produces 500 horsepower (15.8 hp/t) for 50 km/h forward. The D-10S fires OF-412 and BR-412B from 24 rounds; the vehicle has no machine gun, leaving the crew reliant on the main gun. The gunner’s 4x telescope has no rangefinder or stabilizer.
