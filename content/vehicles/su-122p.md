# SU-122P

## Description

The **SU-122P** is a Tank Destroyer that mounted the **122mm D-25T** gun — the same as on the IS-2 — on the T-34 chassis in a limited run during the Second World War. In-game it is a tracked vehicle weighing 31.6 tonnes with a crew of four (driver, gunner, commander, loader). The **Model V-2-34** diesel produces 500 horsepower (15.8 hp/t) for 50 km/h forward. The D-25T fires BR-471 and OF-471 from 25 rounds; the commander has a stabilised **12.7mm DSHK** cupola mount for anti-aircraft and close defence. The gunner uses a 4x telescope with no rangefinder or main-gun stabilizer.
