# SU-85M

## Description

The **SU-85M** is a Tank Destroyer, a Soviet assault gun that combined the improved hull of the T-34-85 with the **85mm D-5S** gun in a fixed casemate; it served from 1944 as a stopgap until the SU-100 entered production. In-game it is a tracked vehicle weighing 31.6 tonnes, crewed by four — driver, gunner, commander, and loader. The **Model V-2-34** diesel produces 500 hp for 15.8 hp/t and 55 km/h.\
Armament is the **85mm D-5S** only — no machine guns — with a simple 2.5x telescope sight and no stabilizer or FCS. The vehicle trades anti-infantry capability for a lower profile and respectable anti-armor performance.
