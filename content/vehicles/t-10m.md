# T-10M

## Description

The **T-10M** is a Heavy Tank and the final production variant of the Soviet IS-8 / T-10 series, which succeeded the IS-3 and served as the Red Army's last dedicated heavy tank into the 1960s. The M modernization added a two-plane stabilizer, improved **122mm M-62-T2S** gun, and infrared equipment. The 50-tonne tracked vehicle is crewed by four and powered by a **V12-6B** diesel producing 750 horsepower for 15 hp/t and 50 km/h.\
The main armament fires APDS (C) (3BM-11), HEAT (3BK-9), AP (BR-472), and HE (OF-472) with a 10-second reload; a coaxial **14.5mm KPVT** provides secondary fire. The gunner has an FCS and 3.1x / 8x magnification but no laser rangefinder. An optional Falanga ATGM addon can be fitted for guided engagement. The T-10M combines heavy armour and a powerful 122 mm gun with mechanical lead computation for improved accuracy.
This vehicle features a single addon: **Falanga ATGM**. The Falanga ATGM addon costs 750 points and equips the T-10M with the 9M17M MCLOS ATGM. This addition significantly boosts the tactical versatility of the already formidable T-10M by allowing it to engage targets behind cover or at extreme ranges where the primary kinetic rounds might lose effectiveness.
