# T-15 Armata

## Description

The **T-15** is an Infantry Fighting Vehicle and the heavy IFV of the Russian Armata family, sharing the same chassis and powertrain lineage as the **T-14 Armata** main battle tank. It was designed to transport and support dismounted infantry while carrying substantial firepower and protection. The 41-tonne tracked vehicle carries a crew of three plus eight passengers and is powered by a **12N360** diesel producing 1,500 horsepower for 36.6 hp/t, with a top speed of 80 km/h in both directions, neutral steering, and an automatic gearbox.\
The turret mounts a stabilized **30mm 2A42M** autocannon, a **7.62mm PKT**, and **9M133 Kornet** ATGMs, with laser and missile warning systems, smoke grenades, and an APS with 10 launchers (70–2,000 m/s interception). The vehicle also has an engine smoke system and optional Kinzhal turret addon.
