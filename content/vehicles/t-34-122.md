# T-34-122

## Description

The **T-34-122** is an Assault Gun built on a T-34 chassis by mounting a **122mm D-30** howitzer in place of the standard turret, creating a makeshift but potent direct- and indirect-fire platform. The D-30 is the same ordnance used on the 2S1 Gvozdika and many towed artillery pieces. The 33-tonne tracked vehicle is crewed by four — driver, gunner, bow gunner, and loader — and powered by a **Model V-2-34** diesel (500 hp, 15.2 hp/t) for 55 km/h.\
The **122mm D-30** fires HE and HEAT (3OF56 and 3BK-10) with a 22-second reload; the gunner has a 3x sight and no stabilizer or rangefinder. A bow **7.62mm DT-28** provides close-in defense. The T-34-122 sacrifices turret flexibility and rate of fire for heavy caliber and hull-down fire support.
