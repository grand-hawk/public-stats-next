# Abandoned Tank Hull

## Description

The **T-54NT** is represented in-game as an abandoned or stripped T-54 hull pressed into use as a light armoured platform. In this form it is classified as an Armoured Personnel Carrier with a single driver and no turret or main armament. The 20-tonne tracked chassis retains the **ChTZ V-54** diesel (520 hp) from the original T-54, yielding a high 26 hp/t and 50 km/h forward speed.\
The only armament is a **7.62mm DT-28** machine gun in the driver's position. With minimal crew and no heavy weapons or armor package, the vehicle functions as a fast, low-signature transport or utility platform rather than a combat tank.
