# T-64BV

## Description

The **T-64BV** is a Main Battle Tank, an upgraded Soviet T-64B with add-on explosive reactive armour (ERA) and improved fire control, fielded from the 1980s. The T-64 series was the first Soviet MBT with an autoloader and composite armour. In-game it is a tracked vehicle weighing 44.1 tonnes with a three-man crew — driver, gunner, and commander. The **HPZ 5TDF** diesel produces 700 hp for 15.9 hp/t and 61 km/h.\
Main armament is the **125mm 2A46M-1** with autoloader (APFSDS, HEAT, HE, and **9M112** ATGM), plus coaxial **7.62mm PKT** and commander **12.7mm NSVT**. The gun is stabilized; sights include laser rangefinder, FCS with lead, and Gen 3 white thermals. Smoke grenades and engine smoke complete the defenses.
