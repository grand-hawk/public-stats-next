# T-64E

## Description

The **T-64E** is a Main Battle Tank and an export-oriented modernization of the T-64, adding heavy auxiliary armament and improved sights to the Ukrainian-upgraded chassis. The T-64 family was developed in Kharkiv and saw extensive service with Soviet and later Ukrainian forces; the E (export) variant was offered with optional **Duplet** ERA and a formidable mix of cannon and machine guns for urban and anti-infantry roles. Crewed by three, the 45-tonne tracked vehicle is powered by an **HPZ 5TDFM** diesel (850 hp, 18.9 hp/t) and reaches 61 km/h.\
Main armament is a stabilized **125mm 2A46M-1** with autoloader (3BM42 APFSDS, **9M112** Kobra ATGM, 3BK18M HEAT, 3OF26 HE), with a laser rangefinder, FCS with lead, 3.9x–9x optical sight, and a Gen 2 thermal sight. The gunner also has a **30mm AGS-17** grenade launcher and a **12.7mm NSVT**; the commander's station mounts a **23mm GSh-23L** autocannon. Smoke grenades and engine smoke are fitted; the commander has a thermal CITV with 1x–12x zoom.
