# T-72B1

## Description

The **T-72B1** is a Main Battle Tank and an upgraded T-72B variant that retains the base hull and turret while adding improved powerplant, thermal optics, and an upgraded autoloader. The T-72B entered Soviet service in the mid-1980s; the B1 designation originally indicated a version without the 9K120 Svir ATGM system, and later modernizations such as the 2022 configuration have added thermals and the **ChTZ V-92S2F** engine. Crewed by three, the 46-tonne tracked vehicle develops 1,130 horsepower for a 24.6 hp/t ratio and a top speed of about 61 km/h.\
The stabilized **125mm 2A46M** is fed by an autoloader (3BM59 APFSDS, 3BK18M HEAT, 3OF26 HE) and is served by a laser rangefinder, fire control system, 8x optical sight, and a Gen 1 thermal sight. A coaxial **7.62mm PKT** and a commander's **12.7mm NSVT** provide secondary fire; smoke grenades and engine smoke are available.
