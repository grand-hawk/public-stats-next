# T-80BVM

## Description

The **T-80BVM** is a Main Battle Tank and a modernized variant of the Soviet-era T-80 series, upgraded for Russian service in the 2010s with improved armour, fire control, and the same gas-turbine powertrain that distinguishes the T-80 family from diesel-powered T-72s and T-90s. It has seen operational use with Russian forces and retains the T-80’s reputation for high road speed. Operated by a crew of three on a tracked chassis weighing 47 tonnes, it is powered by a **GTD-1250** gas turbine producing 1,250 horsepower for a 26.6 hp/t power-to-weight ratio and a top speed of 70 km/h.\
The main armament is a stabilized **125mm 2A46M-1** cannon fed by an autoloader with a 6-second cycle, capable of firing APFSDS, HEAT, HE, and the **9M119M1** laser-guided missile; it is paired with a laser rangefinder, fire control system with lead and tracking, and a second-generation thermal sight with 4x and 12x zoom. A coaxial **7.62mm PKT** and a roof-mounted **12.7mm NSVT** provide secondary armament, and both the main gun and commander’s cupola have stabilizers. Defensive equipment includes smoke grenades and an engine smoke system; gun elevation is limited to -5° to +15°, giving poor depression over frontal terrain.
This vehicle features five addons: **3BM59**, **Anti-Drone Cage**, **ПНМ-Т + TKN-3MTP**, **External Fuel Tanks** and **Model 2023 Conversion**. The 3BM59 addon costs 250 points and replaces the stock 3BM60 with 3BM59 Depleted Uranium (DU) APFSDS; although it technically loses 2 mm of maximum penetration (dropping from 580 mm to 578 mm), it features a 2x spall multiplier that vastly increases post-penetration damage within the enemy fighting compartment. The Anti-Drone Cage costs 150 points and provides a "cope cage" over the turret to prematurely detonate or deflect FPV drone munitions. The ПНМ-Т + TKN-3MTP addon costs 150 points and swaps the stock Gen 2 optics for Gen 3 thermals to offer superior visual clarity and easier target spotting. The External Fuel Tanks addon is free and serves a purely aesthetic purpose, mounting decorative fuel barrels to the rear of the hull for a more authentic and rugged battlefield appearance. Finally, the Model 2023 Conversion costs 500 points and adds extra ERA to the hull sides to bolster protection against chemical rounds and ATGMs,.
