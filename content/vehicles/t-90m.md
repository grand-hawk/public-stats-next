# T-90M

## Description

The **T-90M** is a Main Battle Tank and the latest major upgrade of the Russian T-90 series, with a new turret, improved armor, and modernized fire control; it has been in production since the 2010s and is used by Russian and allied forces. Weighing 50 tonnes with a three-man crew, it is powered by a **ChTZ V-92S2F** diesel producing 1,130 horsepower for 22.6 hp/t and reaches 61.33 km/h forward (reverse remains 4 km/h, typical of Soviet-style transmissions).\
Main armament is the **125mm 2A46M-5** smoothbore with an autoloader (6.8-second cycle), firing APFSDS, HEAT, HE-Frag, and **9M119M1** ATGMs. A coaxial **7.62mm PKT** and a commander’s **12.7mm KORD** complete the armament. The T-90M has a laser warning system, stabilizer, smoke grenades, and engine smoke; the gunner and commander both have laser rangefinders, second-generation thermals, and fire control with lead and tracking.
