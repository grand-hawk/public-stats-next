# T-V

## Description

The **T-V** is a Medium Tank representing a captured German **Panther** (Soviet designation T-V) in Soviet or allied use; the Red Army fielded a number of repainted Panthers during the Second World War. In-game it is a tracked vehicle weighing 44.3 tonnes with a five-man crew and a top speed of 46 km/h forward and 3.5 km/h in reverse. The **MB HL230 P30** engine produces 600 horsepower for 13.5 hp/t and neutral steering.\
Main armament is the **75 mm KwK42** L/70 with a 7.84-second reload and HE, PzGr 39/42, and PzGr 40/42 ammunition; secondary weapons are coaxial and bow **7.92mm MG34** machine guns. The bow position is stabilized; the main gun has no stabilizer. The gunner uses a 2.5x and 5x telescope with no rangefinder. The T-V offers Panther-level firepower and mobility from the opposite side of the matchup.
