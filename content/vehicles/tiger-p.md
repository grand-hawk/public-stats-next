# Porsche Tiger

## Description

The **Tiger P** is a Heavy Tank based on the Porsche-designed competitor to the Henschel [[/vehicles/tiger-i Tiger I]], formally the VK 45.01 (P); Henschel’s design was chosen for production, and most Porsche hulls were later converted into the Ferdinand tank destroyer. The Tiger P uses a petrol-electric drive with twin **Porsche Type 101** engines totaling 612 horsepower, giving 10.2 hp/t; at 60 tonnes with a five-man crew it reaches 31 km/h in both forward and reverse with neutral steering.\
Armament matches the production [[/vehicles/tiger-i Tiger I]]: the **88 mm KwK36** cannon with a 7.84-second reload and two **7.92mm MG34** machine guns (coaxial and bow). The vehicle has smoke grenades but no main-gun stabilizer; the gunner’s sight is a 2.5x telescope. The Porsche Tiger’s complex drivetrain and rear-mounted turret made it a maintenance liability in reality, a trait often reflected in its portrayal in games.
