# GAZ Tigr M

## Description

The **GAZ Tigr M** is a wheeled Transport Vehicle based on the Russian GAZ Tigr (Tiger) multipurpose armored vehicle, in widespread use with Russian and allied forces since the 2000s for reconnaissance, patrol, and troop transport. Weighing 7.2 tonnes, it carries a driver, gunner, and five passengers and reaches 140 km/h on roads and 25 km/h in reverse. The **YaMZ-5347-10** diesel produces 215 horsepower for a 29.9 hp/t power-to-weight ratio.\
Armament is a single **7.62mm PKT** machine gun in a stabilized turret with smoke grenades; the sight is a simple 1x optic with no rangefinder or thermals. The Tigr prioritizes speed and payload over armour, typical of light tactical vehicles in the transport role.
