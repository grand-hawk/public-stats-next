# Type 10

## Description

The **Type 10** is a Main Battle Tank developed by Japan and entering JGSDF service from 2012, designed for domestic terrain and rapid deployment with a focus on firepower and mobility. It is a tracked vehicle weighing 44.4 tonnes with a three-man crew (driver, gunner, commander) and an active suspension that allows ride-height and pitch adjustment. The **8VA 34WTK** diesel produces 1,200 horsepower for a 27 hp/t ratio and a top speed of 70 km/h in both forward and reverse, with neutral steering.\
Main armament is the **120mm TKG** smoothbore fed by an autoloader with a 3.4-second cycle, plus a coaxial **7.62mm Type 74** and a commander’s **12.7mm M2**. The Type 10 has a laser and missile approach warning system, stabilizer, smoke grenades, and a fire control system with laser rangefinder, lead and tracking, and third-generation thermals on both gunner and commander sights.
