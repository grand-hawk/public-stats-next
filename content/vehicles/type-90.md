# Type 90

## Description

The **Type 90** is a Main Battle Tank (Japanese designation Type 90 Kyū-maru), developed by Mitsubishi Heavy Industries for the Japan Ground Self-Defense Force and entering service in 1990; it was Japan’s first modern MBT with a 120 mm smoothbore, autoloader, and advanced fire control. In-game it is a tracked vehicle weighing 50.2 tonnes with a crew of three — driver, gunner, and commander — and features active suspension with adjustable ride height and pitch. The **MHI 10ZG32WT** diesel produces 1,500 horsepower for 29.9 hp/t and a top speed of 73 km/h forward and 34 km/h backward, with neutral steering and an automatic gearbox.\
Main armament is the stabilized **120mm Type 90 L/44** cannon with an autoloader, plus a coaxial **7.62mm Type 74** and a commander’s **12.7mm M2** machine gun. The turret is fitted with a laser rangefinder, fire control system with lead and tracking, Gen 1 green thermals, 1x/10x zoom, laser warning system, and smoke grenades.
