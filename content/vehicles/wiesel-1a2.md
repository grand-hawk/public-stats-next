# Wiesel 1A2

## Description

The **Wiesel 1A2** is a Tank Destroyer built on the same Wiesel 1 chassis as the 1A0 and 1A4, developed for German airborne and light forces to provide anti-tank capability in a tiny, air-transportable package. The 1A2 replaces the 20 mm cannon with a **BGM-71A TOW** missile launcher for long-range engagement of armour. In-game it is a tracked vehicle weighing 2.9 tonnes with a crew of two — driver and gunner — and is powered by an **Audi 2.1 diesel** producing 87 horsepower for 30 hp/t and a top speed of 80 km/h forward and 25 km/h backward, with neutral steering and an automatic gearbox.\
The gunner has Gen 1 white thermals and 3x/13x zoom but no laser rangefinder or stabilizer; the vehicle carries eight TOW rounds and relies on standoff range and mobility rather than armour or smoke.
This vehicle features two addons: **TOW2A/TOW2B** and **Airdrop**. The TOW2A/TOW2B add-on costs 500 points and replaces the stock BGM-71C I-TOW with the BGM-71F TOW 2 series. This upgrade introduces two distinct missile variants: the TOW 2A, which features a tandem warhead and an additional 200mm of penetration (from 800mm to 1000mm), and the TOW 2B, which utilizes a "top-attack" flight profile to strike the thinner roof armor of enemy vehicles with 150mm of penetration. The Airdrop add-on costs 2,000 points and allows the vehicle to deploy at almost any location on the map, enabling flanking maneuvers or the capture of distant enemy objectives.
