# Willys MB

## Description

The **Willys MB** is a Transport Vehicle — the iconic American World War II jeep, produced by Willys-Overland and Ford in huge numbers from 1941 and used by US and Allied forces in every theater. Its simplicity, agility, and four-wheel drive made it a universal runabout for personnel, reconnaissance, and light weapons. In-game it is a wheeled vehicle weighing 1.2 tonnes with seating for three — driver, gunner, and one passenger — and is powered by an **L134** engine producing 60 horsepower for a 50 hp/t power-to-weight ratio and a top speed of 89 km/h forward and 15 km/h backward.\
The gunner operates a stabilized **12.7mm M2** machine gun on a ring mount with 1x optics; the vehicle has no armor to speak of and relies on speed and small size to avoid engagement.
