# WZ-122

## Description

The **WZ-122** is a Main Battle Tank representing a family of Chinese prototype designs from the late 1960s and 1970s that explored a 120 mm main gun, composite armour, and missile capability before the Type 99 lineage; the program did not enter series production. In-game it is a tracked vehicle weighing 37.5 tonnes with a crew of four — driver, gunner, commander, and loader — and is powered by a **CNGC 12150L** diesel producing 650 horsepower for 17.3 hp/t and a top speed of 55 km/h forward and 7 km/h backward.\
Main armament is the stabilized **120mm Cannon Type 71**, supplemented by a coaxial **7.62mm Type 59**, a loader-operated **12.7mm Type 54** machine gun, and a **302** anti-tank missile launcher with four rounds. The gunner has a fire control system and 3.5x/7x zoom on the main sight and 8x on the ATGM sight but no laser rangefinder or thermals; an engine smoke system is fitted.
