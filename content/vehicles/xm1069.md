# XM1069

## Description

The **XM1069** is a Self Propelled Anti Aircraft vehicle based on the MIM-146 [[/vehicles/air-defense-anti-tank-system ADATS]] (Air Defense Anti-Tank System), a Swiss–American development that combined radar-guided surface-to-air missiles with anti-tank capability on an M113-style chassis; the system was trialed by the US and Canada but never widely fielded. In-game it is a tracked vehicle weighing 25 tonnes with a crew of three — driver, gunner, and commander — and is powered by a **VTA-903T** diesel producing 600 horsepower for 24 hp/t and a top speed of 66 km/h forward and 14 km/h backward, with neutral steering and an automatic gearbox.\
The stabilized turret mounts a **25mm M242AA Bushmaster** autocannon, a **12.7mm M3P** machine gun, and an **MIM146** launcher capable of both anti-air and anti-tank missiles. The gunner has a laser rangefinder, fire control system with lead and tracking, and Gen 1 white thermals; an engine smoke system is fitted for defense.
