# XM8

## Description

The **XM8** is a Light Tank developed in the United States as part of the Armored Gun System and later the M8 Armored Gun System program, which sought a lightweight, air-droppable vehicle capable of delivering 105 mm firepower for airborne and light forces. The design never entered full production but influenced later light tank concepts. In-game it is a tracked vehicle weighing 18.5 tonnes, crewed by three — driver, gunner, and commander. The **Detroit 6V92TA** diesel produces 580 horsepower for a strong 31.4 hp/t power-to-weight ratio and a top speed of 72 km/h, with neutral steering and an automatic gearbox.\
Main armament is the stabilized **105mm XM35** cannon fed by an autoloader, with a coaxial **7.62mm M240** and a commander **12.7mm M2**; the gunner sight has a laser rangefinder, fire control system, and Gen 1 green thermals at 3.3x and 10x zoom. Smoke grenades round out the defensive fit.
