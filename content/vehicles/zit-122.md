# ZIT-122

## Description

The **ZIT-122** is a Medium Tank and a mass-production variant of the fictional **ZIT-76** design by Ferrarico, armed with a more powerful 122 mm gun. In-game it is a tracked vehicle weighing 52.4 tonnes and crewed by five — driver, gunner, bow gunner, commander, and loader — with a top speed of 34 km/h forward and 6.2 km/h reverse. A **V-2K** diesel produces 600 horsepower for an 11.5 hp/t power-to-weight ratio.\
Main armament is the **122mm HW-T** with OF-462 HE and BP-460A AP ammunition, 15 s reload, and 14+10 rounds carried; the gunner also has a **12.7mm DSHK** machine gun and a 2.5x sight with no laser rangefinder or stabilizer. The commander has a stabilized cupola with a 12.7 mm DSHK; the loader has a **7.62mm PKT** and a **Grenade Mortar** for frag and smoke; the bow gunner has a stabilized **7.62mm PKT**. The vehicle offers strong firepower and multi-position machine guns at the cost of modest mobility.
